Why are dogs awesome?

Just look at them.

From purebreds to muts dogs help us in many ways. They love us unconditionally, make us laugh, and force us out of the house.

Some helpful information.

Pit bulls have a bad reputation but are very loving and protective dogs. They will forever love you and your family.

Dachshunds are adorable and love to be around you all day. Great for anyone who loves to snuggle. A cute little buddy.

Bull dogs are strong, stubborn, and loving. Great for anyone that doesn't want to walk all day.

Terriers are great at herding your family. If you have kids they will always make sure your kids are were they are supposed to be.
